Dashboard Overview

The dashboard serves as your central command center, providing real-time insights and key performance indicators (KPIs) for your business operations. Here's a detailed breakdown of what you'll find:

Key Metrics
Sales Overview
  • Pending Sale Orders: Track orders awaiting processing
  • Completed Sale Orders: Monitor successfully fulfilled orders
  • Total Receivables: View outstanding payments from customers
Purchase Management
  • Pending Purchase Orders: Monitor incoming inventory orders
  • Completed Purchase Orders: Track received inventory
  • Total Payables: View outstanding payments to suppliers
Business Health
  • Total Expenses: Track all business expenditures
  • Total Customers: Monitor your customer base growth
Interactive Charts and Analytics
Sales vs. Purchases Analysis

The interactive chart displays a 6-month comparison between:

  • Monthly sales revenue from invoices
  • Monthly purchase costs from bills
  • Trend analysis to identify seasonal patterns
Trending Items Analysis

This section helps you:

  • Identify your best-selling products
  • Monitor customer preferences
  • Optimize inventory management
Recent Transactions

The Recent Invoices section provides:

  • Latest transactions
  • Payment status tracking (Paid, Pending, Overdue)
  • Quick access to invoice details
  • Customer information at a glance
Additional Features

Interactive Elements
